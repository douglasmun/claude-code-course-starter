# Connector Integration Notes

## Google Drive

Purpose: retrieve launch docs, customer notes, and approved messaging.

Recommended scope:
- Product launch folder
- Approved brand assets folder

## Slack

Purpose: retrieve team updates and unresolved stakeholder questions.

Recommended scope:
- Product launch channel
- Customer-facing feedback channel

## Context Budget Rule

Connect only the folders and channels needed for the current brief. Disconnect broad workspaces when not actively used.
